Case study Problem defination:


Contoso multinational company wanted to build and host 
"Corporate Customer Accounting Solution" in web.They also want 
 this website to hosted on public cloud Google Platform or AWS
as they have their Offices all over the world and Employee from any 
office can access this information.

--functional requirements:
Descriptions of data to be entered into the system
Descriptions of operations performed by each screen
Descriptions of work-flows performed by the system
Descriptions of system reports or other outputs
Who can enter the data into the system

--non functional requirement:
1.security for the website.
